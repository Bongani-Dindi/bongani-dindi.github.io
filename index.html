Wear Tester

Linear reciprocating wear tester refurbishment

Project Type: Experimental Systems Refurbishment and Automation.

Role: Mechanical Restoration · Embedded System Integration · Vibration Analysis.

Focus: Recommissioning and upgrading a linear reciprocating wear tester

EngineeringSoftware
Details
More

One Eighty Materials Engineering Solutions (Pty) Ltd (OEMES) required the refurbishment of their wear tester units that have experienced mechanical degradation and operate on an old electronic system. The refurbishment aims to repair and replace failed mechanical components, update the electronic motor and sensor control system and reduce the excessive vibration response of the system.
